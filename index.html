Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 14 day free trial work?

+

When you sign up for a 14-day free trial, you get full access to all features of the selected plan. No credit card is required to start. You can cancel at any time during the trial period, with no commitment. At the end of the 14 days, you can choose to continue with a paid plan or your account will be automatically paused.

Can I change plans anytime?

+

Yes, you can change your plan at any time! If you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to additional features. If you downgrade, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. All plan changes are prorated, so you only pay for the difference.

How do I pause my Bonsai subscription?

+

You can pause your Bonsai subscription at any time through your account settings. When paused, you'll keep all your data and documents, but won't have access to premium features until reactivated. There are no fees during the pause period, and you can resume your subscription whenever you want.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. For requests after this period, we review on a case-by-case basis. If you experience issues with our service, our support team is always available to help before considering a refund. Cancelled subscriptions are valid until the end of the billed period.
